Install Dishcovery on iOS

To install Dishcovery on your iPhone or iPad, simply follow these steps:

  1. Open this page in Safari on your iOS device.
  2. Tap the Share button located at the bottom of the screen.
  3. Scroll down and select "Add to Home Screen".
  4. Tap Add in the upper-right corner to confirm.
If you don’t see the ‘Add to Home Screen’ option, please ensure your Safari browser is up-to-date and try again.
