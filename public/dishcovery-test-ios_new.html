Install Dishcovery on Your iOS Device

How to Add Dishcovery to Your Home Screen

To install the Dishcovery app, follow these simple steps:

  1. Tap the Share button (located at the bottom of Safari).
  2. Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen.
  3. Tap Add in the upper-right corner.

Enjoy easy access to personalized recipes anytime!

If you don’t see the ‘Add to Home Screen’ option, ensure Safari is up-to-date and try again.
